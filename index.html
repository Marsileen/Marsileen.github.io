Academics & Ambitions

I am a student at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln (UNL), originally from Lincoln, Nebraska.

I'm pursuing a double major in Global Studies and Journalism, along with a minor in French.

My goal after graduation is to attend law school, with a strong interest in humanitarian law.
